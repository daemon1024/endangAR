Hey! Welcome to EndangAR!

This is a place where you can explore and know more about what's happening in the wilderness! Scroll down to learn how to do this. Find out about the animals and plants in danger all around the world, and you can also explore more with AR by bringing them into your own home and surroundings!





How it works

Step 1: Click on a pin point. A blue point allows you to explore an endangered animal while a black pin point allows you to explore an endangered plant!
Step 2: A pop up displays the name of the plant/animal and some more information about it.
Step 3: Click on the name and it will show you a 3D model which you can also see in your surroundings by clicking the 'See in AR' button. Have Fun m!!
